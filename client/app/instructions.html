Instructions

Ranked Blogs ranks the top blogs in the programming field based on votes by the community and website metrics.

Log In With Facebook

Log in with your Facebook account when prompted by the pop-up to take advantage of all the features of the app, including voting and viewing your favorite blogs.

Click on the Thumbs Up Icon to Vote for a Blog or Remove Your Vote

One click adds your vote and clicking again removes your vote. Blogs you have voted for will appear on your "Favorite Blogs."

Click on "View Favorite Blogs" to Display Your Favorite Blogs

To switch back to view all blogs, click on "View All Blogs."

Click on Column Headers with a Sort Icon to Order Blogs by a Specific Column

Blogs can be ordered by title, MozRank, Page Authority, external links, and Twitter followers.
